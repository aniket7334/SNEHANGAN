Challenges We Address

Problems Faced by Specially-Abled Children

Lack of Digital Tools

Many children face challenges in learning due to the unavailability of modern digital resources such as computers, projectors, or reliable internet access. Without these tools, they are unable to benefit from interactive teaching methods, online educational materials, or digital skill development.

Lack of Teacher Training Programs

Many teachers working with specially-abled children are not provided with proper training programs that focus on inclusive education and special needs teaching methods. Without professional development, they struggle to understand the unique requirements of these children, such as alternative communication techniques, individualized learning strategies, and behavioral management.

Limited or Irregular Funding from Donors

One of the major challenges faced by NGOs is the lack of consistent and adequate financial support. Many organizations depend heavily on donations, which are often irregular and insufficient to cover the growing needs of educational programs, healthcare facilities, and infrastructure. Without stable funding, it becomes difficult to plan long-term projects, retain skilled staff, or provide continuous support to beneficiaries.

Lack of Extracurricular and Skill-Based Learning Opportunities

Children often miss out on opportunities to participate in extracurricular activities such as arts, sports, music, and vocational skill development. These activities play a crucial role in building confidence, teamwork, creativity, and practical life skills. However, due to limited resources and focus only on academics, students are deprived of holistic growth.

Complicated Registration and Legal Requirements

NGOs often face difficulties in navigating complex registration processes and meeting strict legal compliance requirements. The paperwork, lengthy procedures, and frequent changes in government policies create unnecessary delays and barriers for organizations that genuinely want to serve society.
